Asteroids Game: The Blob

Up arrow = Accelerate/Move forwards
Down arrow = Slow down/Move backwards
Left arrow = Turn left
Right arrow = Turn right
S = Hyperspace
Powerups:
Shield: Gain 15 stacks of shielding, protecting you from danger, appears every 45-60 seconds.
Adrenaline: Attacking with bullets increases the bar. When bar is full, click Z to gain healing, faster movement and attack speed.
Annihilation: Fill bar by losing/gaining hp. When bar is full, click X to unleash hellfire in all directions, and a powerful blast afterwards.
(Work in Progress!) Boss: "The Blob" , spawns every two minutes. Has three attacks to throw at you,
but they have a 20 second cooldown before they can use another. Every spawn will increase the hp and difficulty of the boss, good luck!
When HP falls below 0, you enter Overtime (extra time before game ends), immediately gain all powerups.
