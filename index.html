Asteroids Game: The Blob

Up/Down arrow = Move forwards/backwards
Left/Right arrow = Turn left/right
Q = Auto shoot
P = Skip boss spawning time
S = Hyperspace (Teleport to random place)
Powerups:
Shield: Gain 15 stacks of shielding, protecting you from danger, appears every 45-60 seconds.
Adrenaline: Attacking with bullets increases the bar. When bar is full, click Z to gain healing and faster attack speed.
Annihilation: Fill bar by losing/gaining hp. When bar is full, click X to unleash hellfire in all directions, and a powerful blast afterwards.
Boss: "The Blob" , spawns in intervals of 30 seconds. Has two attacks to throw at you,
but they have a 10 second cooldown before they can attack again. Every spawn will increase the hp and difficulty of the boss, good luck!
When HP falls below 0, you enter Overtime (extra time before game ends), immediately gain all powerups.
